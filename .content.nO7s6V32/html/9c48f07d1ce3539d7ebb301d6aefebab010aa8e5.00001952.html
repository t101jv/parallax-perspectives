These are especially urgent now in late February 2021 about several different issues.

 

Urge Biden to rejoin the Iran nuclear deal:

The Joint Comprehensive Plan of Action (JCPOA, commonly known as the “Iran nuclear deal:) was working well until Trump pulled the U.S. out of it and escalated his bullying against Iran — including escalating threats of war against Iran.  Instead of getting “leverage” to force Iran to yield to Trump’s demands, Trump’s scheme backfired.  The Iranian people rightfully feared for their national security, so they elected hardliners, and the government started enriching uranium.  Joe Biden had promised to re-join the JCPOA, but he appointed many war-hawks to powerful positions in his administration, so lately Biden has been wavering and thinking he could apply “leverage.”  We must push Biden to re-join the JCPOA and support peace.

Restorative Justice:

Our criminal justice system is based on punishing people who made mistakes.  A smarter, more compassionate, more effective alternative is “RESTORATIVE JUSTICE,” which recognizes that a crime is about brokenness, so we need to repair the damage and restore what has been broken.  Restorative Justice is better for the victims of crime, and it also helps the person who committed the crime.

Sign up at this link now so you can join this conversation about RESTORATIVE JUSTICE on Thursday March 11, 7:00 pm Pacific Time:  https://www.ipjc.org/restorative-justice-registration/

 

Remove U.S. military troops from the Mexican border:

A few days ago, President Biden formally issued a proclamation to end the fake “national emergency” Trump declared on the U.S.-Mexico border.  However, he has kept 3,600 active duty troops deployed there.  Let’s urge Biden to de-militarize our immigration policies. Urge President Biden to reverse harmful migration policies:

During the campaign and after his inauguration, Joe Biden said many good things, but in many cases he has failed to take the actions he has promised.  We need to urge him to fulfill his campaign promises.  Recently his administration announced it would change U.S. policies to address the root causes of migration from Latin America.  Bold actions are needed in economics and social/political justice and the U.S.’s immigration policies.

SIGN THIS:  Urge President Biden to adopt U.S. policies that address root causes of migration AND respect the rights of migrants and all who need international protection.

 

Protect the U.S. Postal Service!  Replace many of the USPS’s Board of Governors.  Fire Postmaster General Louis DeJoy.

Trump appointed as U.S. Postmaster a political crony (major campaign contributor) who is corrupt (has a private business that competes with the U.S. Postal Service).  Louis DeJoy immediately began sabotaging the USPS in order to slow down the mail and obstruct voting by mail and do other horrible things.  He could be fired by the USPS’s Board of Governors, but many of those were Trump appointees too.  Urge Biden to replace them with honest, competent people and urge them to fire DeJoy.

Tell Biden to STOP the “forever wars”:

For more than 20 years the U.S. has been fighting stupid, unnecessary, unwinnable, counter-productive wars — with no end in sight.  George W. Bush and all presidents for two decades have stretched the Authorizations for Use of Military Force (AUMF) that granted huge powers to fight wars in Afghanistan and Iraq and in dozens of nations around the world.

Now we urge President Biden to use his power as Commander in Chief to

  • Review all military deployments around the world
  • Bring our troops home safely and responsibly as quickly as possible
  • Finally bring the decades-long Forever War to an end.
  • Use the trillions of dollars we spend on endless wars to fund what we really need here in the U.S.

Sign the petition: end the forever war!

 

Urge Congress to use this method to REVERSE MANY OF TRUMP’S CRUEL POLICIES:

The Trump regime AGGRESSIVELY issued rules and deregulations that disastrously hurt immigrants, workers, the environment, and everything else.  An EXISTING federal law allows Congress to fast-track reversal of Trump’s rule-making.  The law is the Congressional Review Act (CRA).

Under the CRA, agencies are required to submit to Congress notice of a finalized rule. Once notified, Congress has the option of passing a joint resolution of disapproval to overturn the rule. If that passes both chambers of Congress and is signed into law by the President, the rule is immediately overturned and has no effect both proactively and retroactively. Importantly, a joint resolution of disapproval need only pass by a simple majority in both chambers.

This means that Congress has the power to negate Trump’s harmful rules.

But there’s a time limit: the CRA only encompasses Trump’s rules created since August 2020 and the new Congress has 60 days to act. That is why we must ask them to act immediately.

Sign the petition: Demand Congress reverse the damage done by Trump and remove his rules under the CRA.
