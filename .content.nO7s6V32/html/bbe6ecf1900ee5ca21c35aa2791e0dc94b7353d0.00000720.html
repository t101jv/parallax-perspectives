Gov. DeSantis’s FEDERAL FELONIES: Kidnapping immigrants + transporting them across state lines

September 27, 2022 GlenAnderson Conservatism and Right Wing, Criminal Justice (Also see Death Penalty), Examples of Parallax Views, Governmental Abuses, Human Rights, Immigrants and refugees, Judicial and Constitutional, Latin America, Our Current Political Crisis (in addition to Trump and Trumpism), Political Parties and Elections (Also see Democracy ...), Race 0

Sign the petition: Ron DeSantis must be investigated.

You read that right. Republican Florida Governor Ron DeSantis may have violated federal law by using over $600,000 in taxpayer dollars to lure 50 asylum seekers onto a flight that diverted them from Florida and sent them to Martha’s Vineyard, Massachusetts. That’s transporting human beings across state lines under false pretenses.

Lawmakers and experts think that DeSantis broke the law. Attorney General Merrick Garland and the Department of Justice cannot stand by and let this continue.

The asylum seekers DeSantis deceived were told they would be given housing and jobs. Instead, they were put on a plane with nothing but a brochure. When the plane landed, many asked “Where am I?”

Among these asylum seekers were families and children. Now is the time to investigate Ron DeSantis for callously using people as pawns in his political agenda.

DeSantis made a premeditated effort to target vulnerable people simply seeking a better life. If he broke the law, he needs to face justice. Sign on to call for the DoJ to start a federal investigation.

 

On Sept. 21, 2022, Alianza Americas (www.alianzaamericas.org) sued Florida Governor Ron DeSantis and others.  This organization of 53 immigrant-led and immigrant-serving community-based organizations believes everyone deserves respect and dignity, every human being has the right to work, and anyone facing an immigration proceeding has the right to legal representation.   On Sept. 20, 2022, they joined forces with asylum seekers who arrived at Martha’s Vineyard as a plaintiff on a federal civil rights class action lawsuit, filed by Lawyers for Civil Rights in Boston, against Florida Governor Ron DeSantis, Secretary of Florida Department of Transportation Jared Perdue, the State of Florida, and their accomplices. The lawsuit challenges the fraudulent and discriminatory scheme to transport nearly 50 vulnerable immigrants, including women and children, from San Antonio, Texas to Martha’s Vineyard.
